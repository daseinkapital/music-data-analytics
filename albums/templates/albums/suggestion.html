Amazon Referral Link

You may be asking yourself what an Amazon referral link is or why you should submit one. According to Amazon's website, if you log in to their affiliate site, you can click on "Product Links" in the "Product Linking" tab, find the item you want, and then generate a link for it. Every time someone clicks your link and buys the product, then you can recieve up to 10% of the purchase back.
